Accept cryptocurrency payments on your WooCommerce website without the need to create an account or complete a lengthy onboarding process.

== Description ==

Seamlessly accept cryptocurrency payments in your WooCommerce store with Sellgate. This powerful plugin offers a hassle-free solution for merchants looking to expand their payment options and tap into the growing crypto market.

Some of our key features include:
- No account registration: Start accepting crypto payments immediately without the need for registration or account setup.
- Low transaction fees: Enjoy minimal processing costs compared to traditional payment methods, maximizing your profits.
- Wide cryptocurrency support: Accept payments in popular cryptocurrencies including Bitcoin, Ethereum, Litecoin, and many more.
- Easy integration: Install and configure the plugin in minutes with our user-friendly interface and step-by-step setup wizard.
- One-click checkout: Customize the checkout process with a single click and select the crypto payment option that best suits your needs.

Unlock the potential of cryptocurrency payments for your WooCommerce store with Sellgate. Expand your customer base, reduce transaction costs, and stay ahead in the evolving e-commerce landscape.

== Frequently asked questions ==

= Do I need to create an account to use Sellgate? =
No, one of the key features of Sellgate is that you can start accepting crypto payments immediately without any account registration or setup process.

= What cryptocurrencies can I accept with Sellgate? =
Sellgate supports a wide range of popular cryptocurrencies, including Bitcoin, Ethereum, Litecoin, and many more.

= Are there any transaction fees? =
Yes, but Sellgate offers low transaction fees compared to traditional payment methods, helping you maximize your profits.

= How long does it take to set up Sellgate on my WooCommerce store? =
Sellgate is designed for easy integration. You can install and configure the plugin in just a few minutes using our user-friendly interface and step-by-step setup wizard.

= Can I customize the checkout process? =
Yes, Sellgate offers a one-click checkout option, allowing you to customize the process and select the crypto payment option that best suits your needs.

= Is Sellgate compatible with the latest version of WooCommerce? =
Yes, Sellgate is regularly updated to ensure compatibility with the latest versions of WordPress and WooCommerce.

= How secure are the transactions processed through Sellgate? =
Sellgate prioritizes security in all transactions. However, for specific security measures, please refer to our documentation or contact our support team.

= Can I use Sellgate alongside other payment gateways? =
Yes, you can use Sellgate in conjunction with other payment gateways to offer your customers a variety of payment options.
